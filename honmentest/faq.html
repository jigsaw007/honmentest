Frequently Asked Questions

What is Saitama Driving Test?
Saitama Driving Test is a platform that provides practice quizzes and resources to help you prepare for the driving license tests in Japan.
How can I use the quizzes?
Simply navigate to the "Quiz" section of our site, select the category you want to practice, and start answering questions!
Are the quizzes free to use?
Yes, all the quizzes and resources on our website are completely free to use.
Can I track my progress?
Currently, progress tracking is not available, but we are working on adding this feature in the future.
Who should use this platform?
This platform is designed for anyone preparing for the Japanese driving license tests, including learners, foreign residents, and experienced drivers looking to refresh their knowledge.
